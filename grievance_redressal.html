ZedPe Grievance Redressal Policy



Introduction

ZedPe (operated by Zedfly Innovations Pvt Ltd ) is committed to providing exceptional service to our customers. We understand that there may be occasions where you encounter issues while using our platform. This Grievance Redressal Policy outlines the process for filing and resolving grievances related to our services.


Who Can File a Grievance?

This policy applies to all users of ZedPe services, including:

  • Merchants using our platform to accept payments.
  • Individual account holders using ZedPe for transactions.
  • Any other party interacting with our platform.

What can be included in a Grievance?

You can file a grievance for any concern related to ZedPe services, including:Issues related to

  • Transactions, such as failed payments, incorrect debits or credits, or unauthorised transactions.
  • Delays in settlements or reversals.
  • Concerns about account security or data privacy.
  • Issues related to ZedPe services, such as functionality problems or technical difficulties.
  • Unprofessional or Rude Behaviour from a ZedPe representative.

How to File a Grievance

There are two ways to file a grievance with ZedPe:


Online Grievance Form: Visit our website and submit a detailed complaint through our online grievance form.

Email: Send an email to our Grievance Officer at grievances@zed.pe with a clear description of your issue.



What information should be included in a Grievance?

When filing a grievance, please include the following information:



Any relevant screenshots, documents, or transaction IDs that support your claim.




How to File a Grievance

  • We aim to acknowledge your grievance within 24 business hours of receipt.
  • A dedicated representative will investigate your complaint and attempt to resolve the issue promptly.
  • You will be kept informed of the progress of your grievance throughout the resolution process.
  • We will strive to provide a fair and reasonable resolution to your grievance within a timeframe depending on the complexity of the issue.

Escalation Process

If you are not satisfied with the initial resolution offered by ZedPe, you have the right to escalate your grievance. You can escalate your grievance by replying to the communication received or contacting our Grievance Officer directly at grievances@zed.pe .


Confidentiality

We will maintain the confidentiality of your grievance and any personal information you provide throughout the resolution process.


Record Keeping

ZedPe will maintain a record of all grievances filed and the resolutions reached.


Revision of the Grievance Policy

We reserve the right to revise this Grievance Policy at any time.  Any changes will be posted on our website.


Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Grievance Policy, please contact our Grievance Officer at grievances@zed.pe .
